Our Story

Founded in 2023 by visionary athlete and entrepreneur Anass Fattah, EXERMO was born from a single mission: to create performance wear that pushes boundaries just as hard as you do. We believe gymwear isn’t just clothing — it’s a mindset, a symbol of resilience, focus, and power.

At EXERMO, every stitch is designed for movement, strength, and confidence. Inspired by athletes, built for warriors, and crafted for anyone ready to go beyond limits.

We’re not just a brand — we’re a movement. Welcome to EXERMO.
